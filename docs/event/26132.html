プレゼンターの自己紹介：
My name is Michael Ntambazi, 23 years old majoring in computer engineering and currently a student intern at GOGA Inc in omotesando Tokyo Japan. I am a Ugandan by nationality, Uganda is located in East Africa and it considered the pearl of africa because of its good climate, hospitable and kind people and its possession of many natural and cultural sites to see.

I would like to share with you the facts about Africa and my country in a simple chat this thursday at GOGA Inc, I will be very glad if you spared some time to have a chat with a Ugandan boy in Japan.

Arigatou gozaimasu.
