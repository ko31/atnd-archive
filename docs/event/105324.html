Homemade DIY Eye Cream Recipes

1. DIY Aloe Vera And Grapeseed Oil Eye Cream

This simple cream contains antioxidant compounds which help in restoring your skin cells and protect them from premature deterioration. The utilization of aloe vera and grape seed oil increase the production of collagen and prevent lines in the area around the eyes.

Ingredients
3 tablespoons aloe vera gel (45 g)
1 teaspoon of grapeseed oil (5 g)
How To Prepare
Insert the aloe gel in a bowl and mix it with grapeseed oil.
How to apply
After washing your face in the evening, apply creams around the eyes and gently rub.
Then massage for a few minutes, until the cream is completely absorbed.
Repeat it every day.
2. Homemade Apple And Honey Eye Cream
homemade eye cream recipes

Apple contains tannic acid, that is a type of illuminated substance. Which help reduces the bag under your eyes.

Combining the properties of apple and honey, you can make moisturizing eye cream to protect the delicate skin around the eyes.

Ingredients
1 ripe apple
3 tablespoons of honey (75 grams)
How To Make It
Cook a ripe apple until it turns into a thick liquid
Mix it in honey and place it in a container, which is airtight
How to apply
The skin around the eyes, where there are dark circles. Put the cream on it for 30 minutes and leave it
Do not leave the remaining cream in the outside or the warm place, it would be better to keep it in a cool place or refrigerator.
And after 30 minutes washed out the cream with warm water
You should use this medicine 2 or 3 times a week
