Unusually high sound

The inspection must be carried out carefully below low-speed operating conditions. Following a speed increase, the sound is mixed using the sound of the rotating sound and the noise entering the steam. Therefore, it is difficult to find the sound source. The abnormal sound is often associated with the vibration, so the sound need to be checked.,find the true reason, and deal from it accordingly.

The increase in bearing temperature is because of bearing metal damage, reduced number of lubricating oil, low lubricating essential oil pressure, high lubricating petrol supply temperature, high rotor vibration worth, and abnormal thrust.
