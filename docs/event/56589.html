IMAメンバー、リアンによるジェネシス・アート・ワークショップは、普通のお絵描き教室とはちょっと違います。
ジェネシス・カード（タロットのように絵にそれぞれ意味のあるリアンのオリジナル・カード。IMAアニバーサリーでは、メンバーやゲストが胸に着けていました/写真）を用い、自分の作
品の中にある心のメッセージを読み取ります。ジェネシスとは「起源」を意味する英語です。
今回のアートはコラージュ。まだあなたの知らない、もっと素敵な自分にアクセス出来るかも？
*日英両言語

Genesis art workshop make an intuitive collage and use the Genesis Cards created by Liane Wakabayashi to find out something new and wonderful about yourself. No art experience necessary. Have fun being creative and discover a message in your collage just for you. ¥1,000 yen. Maximum 12 people.

Art can be used to cultivate intuitive wisdom, whether its been years since you last drew or painted, or yesterday. Genesis Art Workshops are taught by Liane Wakabayashi, Tokyo-based artist, writer and originator of the Genesis Cards (http://www.genesiscards.com/). Liane offers a gentle colorful introduction to creating art from intuition suitable for all ages and levels of art experience. A wide variety of art medium are introduced: painting with European watercolors, poster paints, Neocolor crayons, and pastel techniques, used for creating art from the world before your eyes and the world behind it. Learn how to make connections between art and creativity in daily life through the Genesis Cards, gain extensive practice in Liane’s original ‘unconscious’ indoor and outdoor sketching techniques.

Find out more about Genesis art workshops at www.genesiscards.com
https://www.facebook.com/genesis.art.workshops
