The raddest DJ event meets talk-of-the-town skybar, the BAR at top of Odaiba the SOHO!

Party like a celebrity in an environment clad in designer Masamichi Katayama’s refined interior design as featured on various media, surrounded by breath taking scenery of the night coast. Featuring Special Guest DRUNKEN KONG who debuted at Ageha in 2011!
