It will be an understatement to say that betting industry of UK has prospered over the years to great heights; instead, it has grown exponentially with all potential punters making a comfortable living on the wager money. Horseracing and football is still the mainstay of betting industry in UK.

Betting has been around for a long time and many punters have successfully chanced their hands on one of the most lucrative sport- Football. Football is considered less of gamble and more of science as outcomes can be estimated on team’s performances in that season and past records. Thinking of football, the first thing that comes to the mind is the English premier league that is loaded with fierce teams battling for the prime honor.

No betting system can assure perfect accuracy of match results however, certain betting tips and strategies can definitely increase your chances to win. These tips can ensure you to stay ahead of the race.

League standings

The position on the chart is a good indicator of who is going to have an upper hand. English premier league matches are usually close encounters with draw being most probable result between two equally matched teams. The league standings are best consulted at the middle of the season when the teams have a few matches under their belt and are not tired.

Home or away

Playing home or away is one of the crucial factors that needs consideration before wagering. It has been scientifically proven that the home teams do hold an advantageous position with crowd backing them up. In addition, the away teams play with more caution and strengthening their midfield that makes an away victory highly improbable. Thus, it is better to root for the home team than the visiting team.

Team announcement

The team announcement is one of the major events and it should be followed closely to know who is playing and who is not. The team composition is an easy give away as the strongest teams on paper perform as expected. Thus, you need to keep an eye open for the players’ announcement and keep a track of player injuries etc.

Bookies and sports books

Bookies and sports books are a great source of information. Reliable bookies can give you some good tips about the games that you must keep in mind before betting.

Research and bet right

People tend to bet on their favorite teams, which is not a bright idea. Always keep your emotions aside and wager intelligently keeping in mind the odds, past records and players involved. Teams that are on a roll can be a good bet but never underestimate an underdog.

Sports’ betting has been prevalent from a long time and it is not hard to predict the outcome of matches if you have a good knowledge about the sport. Professional and seasoned punters can easily gauge the situation and predict the outcome most of the times. Even the sports journalists who are constantly on the trot collecting vital information about the teams and the team managers are to some extent abele to predict the result with fair accuracy. Therefore, it can be concluded that betting on sports matches is more of a science combined with some luck.
