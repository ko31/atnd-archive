Qlik Sense – Easy to Use Visualization & Reporting Tool For Business Users. With Qlik Sense, everyone in your organization can easily create flexible, interactive visualizations and make meaningful decisions. Qlik Sense drives insight discovery with a data visualization app that anyone can use.

What is Qlik Sense?
Qlik Sense is a Platform for data analysis. with this Qlik Sense you can analyze data and make data discoveries on your own. You can share knowledge and analyze data in groups and all parts of organizations. Qlik Sense lets you ask and answer your own questions and follow your own paths to insight. Qlik Sense enables you and your colleagues to reach decisions collaboratively.

What can you do in Qlik Sense?
Most of the business Intelligence Products (BI) will help you to answer the questions that are understood in advance. But Coming to the follow-up questions? when some one reads your report or sees your visualization? with this Qlik Sense experience you can answer question after question moving along your own path to insight. with this, you can explore your data freely with just clicks, learning at each step along the way and coming up with next steps based on their earlier findings.
