SSC Result 2017 of all board by Education Board Results of Bangladesh will be published on 2nd week of May, 2017. This Year SSC exam 2017 has been started on 2nd February 2017 with Bangla 1st Paper.
This year, in SSC and equivalent exam 15,43,888 examines taken part from 28,510 educational institutions all over the country. Among them, expected 34,213 boys and 7, 05,645 girls attend in the SSC and equivalent exam at 3,054 centers under 10 Bangladesh education boards.
How to check SSC Result 2017 by SMS mobile?
Get your SSC Result 2017 through you mobile phone by SMS, 1st you have to go to message option and type
SSC/Alim first three letters of your Board name Roll no 2017 and send to 16222
For Example: SSCDHA1536602017 send to 16222
