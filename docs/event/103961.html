In a study conducted recently by their Research Associates with the aim of finding out the status of the number of mechanical engineering assignment experts in and around Australia. The study was conducted to analyse the effect of My Assignment Services on the competing organisations and how their service is better than others.

The mechanical engineering assignment help provided by the experts at My Assignment Services is of an unmatched quality that not only focuses on the technical parameters but on the theoretical aspects as well. This is why other competing organisations are introducing their own online assignment help service in the same subject. In an attempt to ensure that the global reputation of My Assignment Services is not hampered by the upcoming services, the growth was analysed.

According to the report, the number of experts who claimed to be mechanical assignment experts in the year 2017 were 11,789 globally. This number has grown to 24,567 in the year 2018. There has also been a significant rise in the number of students enrolled in a mechanical engineering course and pursuing part time jobs. This number has grown to 5,768 in 2018 from 3,768 in 2017.

These students are the ones who are looking for online mechanical engineering assignment help for their respective colleges. Though a rise in the number of services offering assignment help for mechanical engineering students has been observed, the quality for which My Assignment Services is known for is still unmatched. “I have been taking assignment writing guidance from the experts for past 2 semesters. I am never trusting some other provider ever again.”, says Justin Morris, a student at the Deakin University.
Speaking on the noticed rise, Mr Dylan Dollar, Public Relations Head at My Assignment Services, says, “It doesn’t matter if there is a 5% increase in competition or a 50%. The students know what we are capable of they return to us for the same quality that is same every time.”
