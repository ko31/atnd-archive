description

Your QuickBooks password keeps your account secure and protects your financial data. You can follow the steps given below to change your password in QuickBooks online:
• Step 1: Go to QuickBooks Self-Employed on your browser
• Step 2: Click on the small gear icon at the top of the page
• Step 3: Navigate to ‘Profile’ and select the edit option next to ‘Password’
• Step 4: Complete the instructions to create a new QB password
If you have any problem changing your password you can always call the QuickBooks support number and talk to a trained software technician to know how to proceed.
