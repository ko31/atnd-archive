IBM BPM certification is intended for BPM application developers who lead and contribute to the delivery of complex level process applications. This certification gives attendees the knowledge and confidence to take full advantage of the IBM BPM platform. The practical hands-on learning approach followed at TekSlate will ensure you pass the exam and get job ready by the end of it.

IBM BPM Training, IBM has acquired Lombardi Software in January 2010 and integrated their excellent BPM into a new solution called IBM BPM. IBM BPM comes in three variants: Express, Standard and Advanced. They are different by licensing and products that are included into them. Express comes with limited users and deployment options with Lombardi part only, Standard comes with Lombardi part of BPM and Advanced are integrated with Lombardi and WPS that has advanced service integration, BPEL and ESB.
