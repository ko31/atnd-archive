TI9 Hoof Stomp in Tokyo

This year’s DOTA 2 world tournament, The International, will be held in Shanghai at the Mercedes-Bentz Arena.
This year, after hearing that the normal Pub Stomp wouldn’t be happening, we decided to hold our own fan-version. We rushed around like Centaur Warrunner trying to find a new place for the event. The end result being the TI9 Hoof Stomp in Tokyo. With the help of SolxS Inc. and Gaming Support Team Kamu-e (enocinoco & Kuri9), we were able to put together a public viewing event for the Grand Finals!

The event title comes from the Centaur Warrunner skill, “Hoof Stomp”. Our Pub Stomp event title is a ply on the skill name.
This time, we were “stunned” by events, but the name also represents running around to put things together after the stun, as we were able to do with this event.

We hope this will be a relaxed event where everyone can enjoy watching the TI tournament in an “at home” atmosphere like enocinoco’s channel has.

Get more information at our event site.
