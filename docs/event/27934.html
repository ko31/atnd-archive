イベント ユーザー
event_id 27934
title Japan Satellite 2012
catch Bridging Startups into South East Asia
description

The Japan Satellite is organized by e27 and acts as a lead up event to Echelon 2012. This is the final leg of our 6 Country Satellite search for some of Asia’s hottest tech startups. These startups will pitch their idea to a panel of judges in hopes of securing a spot at the Echelon 2012 Startup Marketplace.

Besides the pitching sessions, there will be keynote sessions covering the various South East Asia startup eco-system. We warmly invite you If you are interested about markets such as Singapore, Indonesia and Vietnam.

A summarized agenda of the event can be found here as well:

10.00am : Opening remarks by e27
10.10am : Opening Speech by Mr Mikihiro Yasuda, Open Network Labs
10.20am : Opening Speech by Mr Yuma Saito, Business Development Division Director, Tohmatsu Venture Support
10.30am : Keynote – Case study of a successful Startup Pivot by Simon Newstead
10.50am : Keynote by Mr Yasuhiko Yurimoto, Global Brain
11.10am : Startup Pitches 1
12.30pm : Lunch
1.30pm : Keynote, The BlackBerry Opportunity by Alan Wong
1.50pm : Keynote by Sandeep Cassi
2.10pm : Keynote – An overseas experience for Japanese startups by Hiromichi Ando
2.30pm : Startup Pitches 2
4.00pm : Results and announcements
