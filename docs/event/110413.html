Tracking control method

The pursuing control system uses two tracking controls. One is light manipulate, which uses a light sensor. The position with the sun is judged according towards the difference in light intensity between different aspects of the sky, and then this motor is rotated to observe. The second is time manage. According to the local latitude plus longitude coordinates and time, the astronomical formula is employed to calculate the coordinates on the sky where the sun can be found, and then the motor is actually rotated to track.



At present, domestic companies combine two command principles, with time control as being the mainstay and light control because the supplement. That is to say, when the weather is great, use the time control to track the approximate position of the sun, and then use the light control to produce precise adjustment; if the climate are not good, use time control alone to track plus avoid the sky stray gentle interference. In addition, another combination is usually adopted: still based on moment control, supplemented by light handle. That is to say, in the case of good weather, simply use mild control to track; if the item encounters rainy weather, it will automatically jump on the time control mode for checking. In the time control style, the GPS module is helpful to obtain the local latitude and also longitude and time. Accuracy of coordinates and time is certain to improve tracking accuracy.
