【MOBA-HUB 13】in TOKYO

”MOBA-HUB”is an off-line meetup event produced by the gaming support team Kamu-e（カムイ）. It is a DOTA 2-centered event to share hints and tips, exchange information, and generally enjoy talking with other game lovers. While DOTA 2 is one of the main topics, participants are more than welcome to bring up any other games you have been playing recently and share other gaming news as well. We aim for a relaxed atmosphere where everyone can have fun while sharing in one of our favorite hobbies.

This time, the MOBA-HUB event will take place before the Tokyo Pubstomp 24 Hour DOTA 2 Finals viewing party, and we will be moving to the Pub Stomp event after dinner.

This time we decided on yaki-nikku after it was the overwhelmingly popular option on our Twitter survey.
It will be 120 minutes of all-you-can-eat yaki-nikku and 92 other food options. Drinks will be paid for seperately.

　
To get more information easily or contact us on the day of, please be sure to Follow @enocinico
