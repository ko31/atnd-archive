A Happy New Year! Let’s do challenge yourself with something new.
“Try on a Kimono!!”

By wearing kimono, you can put yourself in an extraordinary world. In
addition, you can acquire culture and beauty. It’s a little challenging to wear
a kimono by yourself, but we will give you the lesson in a careful and
friendly manner. It is 120 minutes when your new world will begin.
