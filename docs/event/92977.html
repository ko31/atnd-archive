Information in English

MOBA-HUB is an off-line meeting of gamers sponsored by Gaming Support Team Kamu-e. While it mainly revolves around DOTA2, anyone with a game they would like to share is welcome! It’s an event with a relaxed atmosphere where anyone can talk about games, share strategies, or just make some new friends to game with!

This time, MOBA-HUB will partner with enocinoco. Any viewers from enocinoco’s channel are encouraged to come join. You don’t have to be a DOTA 2 player. We hope everyone who can will come join us in having fun and closing out the year!
  • We want to know how many people will likely come for reservations, so please press “参加” even if you aren’t 100% sure.

To keep up with the latest information, please follow enocinoco on Twitter. Anyone following on Twitter are welcome to join!
