Let’s send our prayers and messages of encouragement for the people of Japan. We hope to send 200,000 messages from all over the world, the same number as the number of people who lost their lives, are missing or evacuated as a result of the earthquake, tsunami and nuclear disaster.

Only Two Simple Steps to Help Japan

1: Send your message now!
www.ganbare-nippon.org.au

2: Tell your family and friends!

Five Easy Ways to Post Your Messages

1: Visit: http://www.ganbare-nippon.org.au
Please go to Message Form form top right.

2: Post via Facebook: http://www.facebook.com/pages/Ganbare-Nippon/195072690530883
Please click “Post Message” tab.

3: Post via Twitter: http://twitter.com/StayStrongJapan
Please tweet your message for Japan.

4: Sent via Email: 200000messages@heartojapan.org:
Please attach your small picture if you wish!

5: Ask people to write their messages on notebook and tell us about it!
Please ask for Messages of Hope for Japan at your events. Perhaps you could place notebooks in your restaurant, cafe, shop or office etc.
