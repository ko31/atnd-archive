お部屋と心をキラキラ✨にするサンキャッチャーをご存知ですか。
窓辺に吊るし太陽の光を通して虹色の光を生み出す素敵なインテリア装飾品です。
レインボーメーカーとも呼ばれています。
Suncatcher/Rainbow maker is interior decoration to make your room lovely.
When a suncatcher is placed in direct sunlight or near a bright light, it disperses that light into colorful rainbows and light flecks that scatter around the room. Suncatchers have been used by many to bring good Feng Shui into the home or workplace.

We offer real Swarovski crystal and several gemstones for your suncatcher. Please ask Ms.Atsuko about meaning of each gemstones and find your stones at workshop.
You can also make gemstone accessories with Ms. Atsuko after workshop.
