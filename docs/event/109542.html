The most creative event offering you to reassess trends and jewelry fashion. You will see a variety of unordinary jewelry and hear a myriad of useful advice on choosing and wearing it.

The engagement ring seems to be the most traditional item that is not the object for experimenting. We just used to perceive it in such way. It is time to think out of the box. Let’s go beyond the existing prejudices and lasting wedding conceptions. GS Diamonds jewelers developed an extremely innovative approach to jewelry design and have a lot to demonstrate.

Queen Victoria building has never been as creative as it will be these days. You should see this with your own eyes. New collection of challenging engagement rings, fresh vision of wedding looks, unexpected fashion advice and the best quality you have ever happened to observe.

You will have a unique opportunity to see that innovative jewelry first, try on yourself the dream specimens and offer your individual vision for possible improvement in the light of new conception. Invite you to make a fashion breakthrough together!
