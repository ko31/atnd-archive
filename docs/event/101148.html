Students and examinations have never been best friends. The rising global competition and international recognition of the Australian universities call for only the best of the students to graduate. Therefore, an efficient way of ensuring the students prefer studying and are able to develop all the essential traits that will help them in the professional world, the assignments are made an integral part of their curriculum. Moreover, the professors are constantly raising the competition standards in the hope that the students will develop their skills and knowledge.
After a thorough discussion and analysis, the Department of Education and Training has declared that these assignments are essential for the students in their curriculum. Daniel Langdon, The Head of Course Design, My Assignment Services says, “Students need to understand that these assignments are for their own benefit. Maybe they are hiring our online assignment help services for guidance in their assignments or writing their assignments, but the idea behind that question file is not changing. When you send us your assignment, then also you are giving it a read, deciding if you can do it or not, explaining us your requirements that what the question file is demanding, these are all skills that you develop without evening realising it.”
Students, however, are not particularly pleased with this. Ann Lynn, an international student pursuing undergraduate in the business administration said, “I am disappointed with this. Had assignment writing services like My Assignment Services not been there, God knows what state I would be in. With their assignments, I could leave my exam preparation until the last day without fear. And what’s more is that they are available 24×7! Me and my friends when used to study in the night and we were stuck in some question, I always used to call them up and talk to an expert. They hardly charged anything for the consultation from what I thought I would be needing to pay. Its been 2 years and I always think they are actually proving the students that Assignment help students excel in their studies.”
