DESCONECTA, 09 Feb.

Valentine’s Day brings with it an infinity of potentially romantic moments, gifts, dinners and, also, some errors. People, like humans, fail at times, and Valentine’s Day seems to be a good day for it.

The most common Valentine’s mistakes can be forget about this date and make it important for the couple, but also think that with a simple gift has already ‘been fulfilled’ enough with this date. This is explained by Mila Cahue, author of the book ’Love of the Good. When you find it, take care of it and enjoy it ’, in which it tries to offer the reader keys to enjoy a healthy relationship.

1. Forget the date

If the couple cares a lot about Valentine’s Day, forgetting this day can be a ‘fatal mistake’. According to the author, Saint Valentine is the perfect excuse to “tell us and remind us that we are with the person we choose” and thus “renew the illusion and prevent the flame of love goes out.”

2. Valentine and nothing else

Worrying just for celebrating Valentine’s Day and forgetting to keep the ‘spark of love’ the rest of the year is another big mistake. Cahue says that although Valentine’s Day is a good opportunity for romanticism, you can not fall into the mistake of forgetting the rest of the year to enjoy with the couple.

3. Plans with friends or co-workers

The year has 365 days, and anyone can be good to stay with friends, family or co-workers. In this sense, the writer says that you should book Valentine’s Day to celebrate as a couple and leave those other activities for another day.

4. Valentine = a simple gift

The celebration of Valentine does not have to involve gifts. This author asserts that basing Valentine’s Day on a single gift is a mistake, since on this date – and, indeed, any time of the year -, the most important thing is to show affection and to have romantic gestures that do not have for Which involves spending money.

5. Buy gifts thinking about yourself

Although gifts are not everything, it is also true that they like to give and receive details. Now, another mistake mentioned by Cahue is to buy a gift thinking about oneself and not the couple. “If you think about the perfect plan to make Valentine’s Day, we must forget what we would really like to do to us,” he explains, while ensuring that the essential thing is to put yourself in the other person’s place and, So, decide the gift you would most like to receive.

6. Do not organize anything

The couple is a thing of two, but sometimes “it is assumed that the other person is going to be the one who gives us the surprise” and chooses not to organize anything while waiting for the initiative of the couple. This, he says, is another common mistake: “in the first years of relationship that may happen, but over time we all get tired of the lack of surprises.” Therefore, it is also good to ‘change the roles’ and organize something for the other person.

7. Lack of originality

Triumph in Valentine brings with it a touch of originality. Give each year the same is not advisable, but it is to leave aside the monotony and “draw the illusion to surprise the couple.” In this sense, Cahue invites to take advantage of the fact that Valentine’s Day is only once a year, so you have to make the most of it.
