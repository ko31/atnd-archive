Hello everyone, you’re invited to attend a Gaijin Game Developers meet in Osaka!

Place: Gyoza Factory Chibisuke. Their 飲み放題(all you can drink) is reasonably priced. ;)




There’s no real formal anything here, let’s get to know each other and drink up, exchange stories, meishi/name cards and have a good time. I highly recommend bringing personal or company meishi. I plan to get some printed up at Graphic.



Feel free to bring guests, just make sure you indicate such so I can reserve the appropriate number of seats.

This event is open to anyone interested in coming; if you have a nihonjin friend that’s interested in attending, please bring them along!



Kansai has a strong presence in the Japanese game dev world so I hope to see lots of people attending!



宜しくお願い致します！
