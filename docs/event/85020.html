United States Postal Service Change Management Service (USPS)

Administrative Agency:

United States Postal Service (USPS)
Description of Program

The Change of Address of the United States Postal Service (USPS) allows users to process a change of address for the forwarding of their mail. This service is very useful for those who have been affected by a disaster and need their mail forwarded to a new postal address.
General Program Requirements

Anyone can use this service if they need their mail forwarded to a new address as a result of a temporary relocation or permanent move.
Request Process

There are two ways to change your address:
Visit the Change of Address Postal Service (in English).

You must:
Confirm your identity through a minimum charge on your credit or debit card.
Have a valid email address.

Complete and submit the form PS Form 3575 at any Post Office of the United States.

Then you can:
Deliver the form directly to the United States Post Office, mail it to any Post Office.
Or hand it over to your postman.
Program Contact Information

For more information, visit Help USPS .

Or call:
1-800-ASK-USPS (1-800-275-8777)
