The marketing mix is the ideas or strategies that are used by the company for the Promotion of their product. It includes 4P’s of marketing that is Price, Promotion, Place, and Product.

 

Price: The value that is assigned to the product is termed as price. The price of the product includes all the expenses right from the production of a product to its distribution.

 

Product: Product refers to the manufactured item. Hence, a quality product can occupy a good market position.

 

Place: Choosing correct places for the promotion of a product is also one of the essential strategies for marketing.

 

Promotion: Advertising the product and choosing a suitable platform to tell consumers about the benefit of using the product comes under the promotional strategy of marketing.
