FROM CHRISTIANE WARNECKE
The new package center in Obertshausen is a prestige project for DHL. It is the largest in Germany and hides in its interior a unique in Europe sorting technology. There’s just one catch: Although the huge building stands since 2013 roll there still no vices. That should change soon.
Photo: Henning Kaiser (dpa)
Obertshausen.
discovered the sandals in the favorite color at Zalando, bought a coveted smartphone at a bargain price on Ebay – Shopping on the Internet is becoming increasingly popular. This drives the retailers in inner cities worry lines on the forehead. The logistics company, however cheer about the trend for shopping on the Internet, because the goods go via parcel or package to the customer, and the boom of the shipping trade leaves at DHL, Hermes and other logistics companies are pouring cash.

In order to handle the rapidly growing business, shoot parcel distribution centers in many places in the Republic of the ground. The largest of its kind available since 2013 in the small Obertshausen. Yet the impact of the city in the Offenbach district, however, are difficult to predict, because the gigantic parcel center of DHL is now indeed a common sight, but it takes only this summer its operation on – much later than planned.

The vices were originally roll on the ramps since, 2015. As the DHL have however decided in the meantime for the entire install new sorting technology itself, the opening is now scheduled for June this year. The announced DHL spokesman Thomas Kutsch talking with this newspaper. The plant is a unique European pilot project. Because the quality is decisive and not the schedule, supplemented coach.

50 000. packages per hour

Early June is planning to implement for the first time to the public an insight into the 40th 000 square meter yellow building the DHL. 50 000. packages per hour are sorted there. The horseshoe-shaped complex to have cost a high double-digit millions, has 330 loading stations for trucks. And it is this amount of truck makes the citizens in the surrounding communities fear.

Already Obertshausen is known to many people mainly through traffic reports on the radio, because around the motorway junction of the A3 occurs in the rush hour every day to disabilities.

What happens then only when there is still classified here hundreds trucks a day in the queues at the traffic lights in front of the motorway exit on the market ?, ask concerned citizens and the authorities in the town halls. However, DHL spokesman Heinz-Jürgen Thomeczek allayed the concerns: "Our trucks are mainly in the very early morning and late at night and on the road at night, so it threatens no tightening of commuter traffic.

An appraisal of the Fachhochschule Kassel, is available to the Road Safety Authority Hessen Mobil and the city Obertshausen, also came to the conclusion that a reconstruction of the traffic lights in front of the access to the motorway makes little sense, because the highway itself was overloaded. A real solution to the traffic problems around Obertshausen could only bring a four-lane expansion of the A 3 and a reconstruction of the Offenbacher cross. And let him be dependent on the federal transport infrastructure planning. By the end of the Bundestag I determine the classification of the urgency of the expansion of A 3, explains Achim Brand of Hessen Mobile.

And Hesse Economics Minister Tarek Al-Wazir (Green) sent a message to an inquiry by the Dietzenbacher Landtag Ulrike Alex (SPD), the municipalities are responsible for the designation of commercial areas and would also ensure an efficient public transport.

Hessen-Mobile spokesman fire closes meanwhile does not exclude that the commissioning of the parcel center leads to further congestion, because according to his knowledge is certainly also with package tours during the occupation times to be expected.

Also Obert Stockhausen First Councillor Hubert Gerhard (CDU) assesses the situation critical: “The motorway is now resolved not optimal,” he admits. The report had revealed, however, that the country road L 3117 can accommodate the extra traffic. is also advantageous that the package vices would not pass through built-up spatial layers to achieve the new distribution center.

In fact, just the convenient location of the package center to the motorway for DHL the big selling point of the new location. It is located in the industrial park Herb fields a few hundred meters from junction Obertshausen away for A 3 on the site of 2003 closed apparatus main depot of the Bundeswehr. The airport and the A 5 are from there only three exits. For logisticians thus almost a dream location.
