R.V. College of Engineering was set up in the year 1963 spread over 51.4 acres on the banks of the Vrishabhavati River in Bangalore. The autonomous institution is affiliated to Visvesvarya Technological University and is approved by NBA, AICTE, New Delhi.
R.V. College of Engineering was ranked 49th engineering colleges in India by the National Institutional Ranking Framework in the year 2017.
