===English=====
The joint year-end party of Geomedia Summit and FOSS4G.


  • Start time：2009/12/21(月) 19:30〜22:00

  • At ：Ebisu Monsoon Cafe (2F)

  • Map: http://www.monsoon-cafe.jp/jp/ebisu/home/location/printer

  • Participation fee：4,000yen (Please pay at reception desk)


Twitter hashtag #off4g
Organizer hal_sk_e

*Geomedia Summit is one of the biggest location-based-service’s industry group with non-commercial based. Usually we have meetings about LBS twice per year but this time is just a meetup party.
*FOSS4G means ‘Free and Open Source Software for Geospacial’. It is the annual conference attracting the best of Geospatial Open Source users and developers organized by OsGeo foundation.
