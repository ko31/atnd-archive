I’d like to welcome all the Perfume Fans from around the world with “OKONOMIYAKI”. Let me and Japanese Perfume fans know how much you love Perfume!

OKONOMIYAKI is thin and flat pancake cooked on a hot plate with bits of meat, seafood and chopped cabbages. It’s a special food of Hiroshima where they were born, raised and formed “Perfume” at the age of 11.
