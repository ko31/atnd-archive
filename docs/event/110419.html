1. The scope of program
Crank type power presses are popular in cutting, punching, blanking, twisting, riveting and forming processes. The metal is plastically deformed and fractured by applying a strong pressure to the metal blank. Such as punching, bending, forming, shallow press, sheet bending machine, shearing machine, automatic chilly heading machine, high speed press, precision press, hot forging push, etc.

In order to conform to the process requirements of distinct parts in production, various types of crank power presses can be used. These presses have their personal unique structural forms and features.



2. Structural form and function characteristics of various kinds of crank type power presses
ONE. 1 Classification according to the process use of the press

According to the course of action, the press can be torn into two categories: general hit and public press. The press might be further divided into sheet push presses, volume die forging clicks and shear presses.

1. 2 Classification based on the fuselage structure of your press

According to the structure of the fuselage, the press can always be divided into two types: open press and closed press.

YOU. 2. 1 Open press

Reported by the structure of the fuselage, the open press is usually divided into two types connected with double-column open presses and single-column available presses; according to the structure belonging to the worktable, it is divided towards two types: fixed bench presses and lift bench presses; Reported by the tilt of the fuselage, it may be divided into two types: tilting hit and non-tilting press.

1. A COUPLE OF. 1. 1 Double column amenable press

The back of the fuselage has an opening, as well as upper part of the fuselage forms two side posts. The crank is supported to the legs on both sides and parallel on the front of the fuselage dining room table. In addition, the fuselage might be tilted along the underframe from the press, so that the stamped piece is usually automatically slid down along the opening slope of the back of the fuselage by its own weight, which is beneficial for the mechanization and automation of this stamping process.

1. 2. ONE. 2 Single split press

The fuselage is hollow and has only 1 upright on the top in the fuselage. The back of the particular press body is closed, plus the press can only feed flat when working. The single-column fixed table structure is generally used for open presses with a large nominal pressure; the single-column lifting table structure can change the closing levels of the press in a number of, and the applicable process range is wide.

1. 2. TWO Closed press

The fuselage from the closed press is a frame structure, the fuselage is open in front and back, and the facets are closed, so that the operator might only approach the workbench in the front and the back. The strength and rigidity in the closed press body are great. Therefore, when the workpiece is usually loaded, the elastic deformation of the fuselage is small, and the effect on the accuracy of the workpiece along with the normal operation of the mold is additionally small, so the closed press is mainly Large and medium-sized presses.

A SINGLE. 3 According to the lots of press linkages

According to the volume of press linkages, the crank type power press is divided in to a single-point press, a double-point touch, a multi-point press, etc. The slider on the single-point press is driven by just a connecting rod, which is generally a smaller pressure. The slider of the particular double-point press is driven through two connecting rods, generally a medium-sized press.
