This meetup is for Google Glass users, enthusiasts and those who are simply curious to learn about this new wave of wearable technology. We will host events to share ideas, experiences and host Q&A sessions about Glass. From Google Glass Pioneers to everyday tweeters who dream, everyone is welcome.

If you want to present or talk about your Google Glass project contact me beforehand.
