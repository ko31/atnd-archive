description

さくら会xSCSNxSCDNxTITANは皆様を星野博士壮行会BBQパーティへとお招き致します。星野博士はオーガナイザーとしてSCSNに多大な貢献をされました．また，さくら会およびTITANを牽引した功労者であります．そんな星野博士は8月末に日本へ戻られることになりました．星野博士の成功を願って祈って，お祝いしましょう．また南カリフォルニアの新しいお友達や同僚の方と親交を深める良い機会になるでしょう．今回はさくら会xSCSNxSCDNxTITAN初の合同企画になります．

日時：　 8月 21日　土曜　１２時～
場所：　 Common area right next to Community Room in Palo Verde
Housing, UC Irvine
(on-campus housing Palo Verde 7000)
会費：　 10ドルくらいを予定
駐車場： コミュニティールームの建物前のゲストパーキングには限りがありますので、大学の体育館ARCに駐車することをお勧め致します（２時間毎に１ドル）。palo
verde housingは体育館から徒歩７～８分の距離にあります。
地図：　 添付されているARCからコミュニティールームへの地図とhttp://www.uci.edu/campusmap/をご参考下さい。

どうぞお気軽にご参加くださいませ。飛び入り参加も歓迎致します。

================

Japanese Students and Scholars Association (JSSA)xSouthern California
Science Network (SCSN)xSouthern California Developer Network
(SCDN)xTokyo Institute Technology Alumni Network (TITAN) invite you to
the “See you Dr. Hoshino” BBQ party. Dr. Hoshino has been making great
contributions to SCSN as one of the main organizers. He also led JSSA
and TITAN. At the end of August, he is going back to Japan. This is
the time to thank Dr. Hoshino and cross our fingers for his future
success. Also it will be a great opportunity to meet new friends and
colleagues in southern California. This will be the first
collaboration between JSSA, SCSN, SCDN, and TITAN.

DATE & TIME: August 21st Saturday, starting at NOON
PLACE: Common area right next to Community Room in Palo Verde Housing, UC Irvine
(on-campus housing Palo Verde 7000)
FEE: $10 (subject to change)
PARKING: Visitors parking spots in front of the building of the
community room are limited so we encourage you to park your car at the
campus gym, ARC (a dollar per every two hours). From there, Palo Verde
Housing is located within a 7-8 min walking distance.
MAP: Attached is a map from the parking lot at the ARC to the
community room. Visit the UCI web site at
http://www.uci.edu/campusmap/

Anyone with interest in the party is warmly welcome so feel free to
join us! Drop-ins are always welcome.
