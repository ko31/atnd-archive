Sitecore is an ASP.Net-based WCM web application technology which houses everything your marketing team needs to store, manage, analyze, and update your website content in a single unified solution. After the initial development of your website using types of ASP.NET MVC, the management ownership is placed in the hands of your marketing team instead of IT to create content, forms, and articles and measure the value of the content throughout its lifecycle.

Web Content Management or WCM is the heart of the Sitecore platform. WCM platforms allow you to build your website on top of a set of tools so that the websites are easier to manage, optimized for multiple languages and platforms, and enables marketing teams to own the customer experience.
