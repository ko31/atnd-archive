==========
TAIYO33OSAKA presents Pre-TAIYO DAIKANSHA Festival
==========

Let’s be thankful towards the TAIYO that is the pinnacle of all
energy generated on earth. Yes, as uneasy as that may sound, there it
is. Maybe its just a translation issue. Anyway, we will be having a
festival slash party in Osaka in early March. This isn’t just any
festival. Our bands will be playing using equipment that uses
electricity generated from solar electricity, and this interesting
bike will generate electricity for the USTREAM staff to use. (you
know, like you get on the bike, ride it, and electricity is born).

In addition to live bands, we will have performances, workshops, and
film screenings. Also food will be served (which is probably the
reason that I would go if I were you, but don’t tell the other staff I
said that or they will chop off my head).

■Time & Date: March 2nd (Fri) and 3rd (Sat)
※For details please check the schedule below
■Place: PineBrooklyn
Fukushima 1-2-35, Fukushima-ku, Osaka
(FYI: No, this place has nothing to do with the nuclear power plant)
※5 minute walk from Fukushima station, Shin-Fukushima station on the
JR line, or Fukushima station on the Hanshin line.
■Admission: Each session 2,012 yen
※Please use our online ticket system to purchase tickets
※Please note that each one must purchase a ticket for each “session”
(we will be clearing out the venue after each session)
http://taiyo33osaka.shop-pro.jp/
■More details at http://taiyo33osaka.net/event/#kiji169
