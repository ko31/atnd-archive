ESM6 (Engineer Startup Meeting 6)
Revealing the IT + Web infrastructures at nanapi, the know-how-website aiming to become the No.1 in the world

About ESM

ESM is a meetup event for IT/Web developers and entrepreneurs, run by Globis MBAs. A keynote speech is conducted by a CEO/CTO from successful startups, followed by 5 min pitches from multiple business plan holders. At the networking party you can communicate with thekey note speaker and the biz plan holders. Meet your future comrades at ESM, who can support you through the competition in the global market!
